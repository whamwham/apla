Our mission

By placing distributed ledger technology at the heart of digital transformation, we increase cyber security, reduce bureaucracy and replace legacy systems. With this, businesses and governments become more efficient.

Smart implementation of blockchain redefines the trust we place in each other, making interactions more transparent, fair and reliable. This is why we will impact on the World Happiness Index, giving everyone access to knowledge, integrating developing countries into the global economy and bringing joy to people’s lives.

Here is our historyDrag it

2011

Oleg Strelenko, the founder of the project, started creating his own cryptocurrency: DCoin.

April 2014

Release of DCoin: a cryptocurrency viable without exchanges, banks and other mediators. 66 types of transactions, 77 currencies, video identification and an integrated exchange system for converting DCoin into cash.

December 2014

DCoin rewritten from PHP to Go (more than 73,000 lines of code). Works on Win (64/32), OSX (64/32), Linux (64/32), FreeBSD (64/32), Android, IOS, supports PostgreSQL, SQLite, MySQL.

January 2016

DCoin attracts its first investor.

June 2016

Work starts on creating the first blockchain platform prototype as part of the Daylight project. The first office opens in Moscow.

October 2016

The first versions of the web system, plus Simvolio and Prototypo programming languages are created. The project is renamed as eGaaS (electronic Government as a Service).

Spring 2017

The test network is launched. The concept and ideology of smart contracts alongside a platform legal system is developed. The proof of concept for the first projects is validated for the UAE, India and Luxembourg.

July 2017

An office is opened in Luxembourg.

August 2017

The platform’s application is expanded so that it may be implemented outside of eGovernment. Apla is born: a team of experts for developing and integrating blockchain solutions. eGaaS is accepted into the Luxembourg centre for fintech.

Autumn 2017

Proprietary products (programming client, Simvolo and Protypo languages) are perfected. The visual editor and builder for interfaces is created.

By 2018

Apla Enterprise Network (AEN) blockchain network is launched for business. The network speed is increased by 5-10 times (to 1,500 tx/s). Offices open in Dubai, Singapore and New York.
