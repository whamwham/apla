Why we use blockchain?

Secure data

Blockchain guarantees that any information can not be arbitrarily amended, altered or or deleted by anybody except those authorised in government.

Transparent

It is easy to track actions made by every system user and define who is specifically responsible for each.

Speed

Operational efficiency is increased hundreds of times.

Key Benefits of the Apla Blockchain

Blockchain 3.0

We use “Smart Law” technology that operate above the level of protocols. Unlike Smart Contracts, Smart Laws can evaluate and rectify errors where there are program contradictions or flaws in the algorithm.

Regulated ecosystem

The blockchain network is private and permissioned, meaning only the government can see, use or alter information; it is also leak-proof.

Rapid development

It took Apla just 8 days to build the entire system. Moreover, an intuitive interface coupled with simple syntax allows to train staff from local agencies easily.

Independence

It gives government complete control over the platform and is also less dependent on the technology provider.

Interface editor

It’s simple to use for any user, even those without a technical background.

Cross-platform

Simple to use from desktop or any Android iOS devices.
