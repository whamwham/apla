Summary

The Government of India is simplifying their services for customers by going digital and using blockchain technology. As part of this drive, in 2017 they decided to reinvent the land registry experience.

After an exhaustive search for the right partner, the Government of Andhra Pradesh chose Apla to design & engineer a new digital platform that could respond to modern needs & behaviors.

We partnered with the Government of India to design and develop the world’s first blockchain land registry proof of concept that meets the needs of modern citizens. We created a working prototype in just 8 days. It was well-received & endorsed by the client. $700 million saved on bribes alone. Background

Bureaucracy in India is a constant for those buying or selling land.

It takes months or even years to register one’s estate; it requires many papers, time & money to do everything. Even then, you could lose money or land since it’s easy to falsify and alter entries in the land registry.

The current system is rife with corruption. It is estimated that $700 million is being paid in bribes at land registrars across India.

J. A. Chowdary
Special Chief Secretary & IT advisor to the Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh

Problems

  • Physical verification of documents takes months.
  • Paper-based documents total up to 3,000 pages per registration process.
  • Property cases take years to solve in court.
  • Multiple authorities for owner verification, property verification, legal status, financial verification & regulatory checks.
  • Payment of stamp duty is recorded manually on a paper ledger.
  • Preparing the documents involves a third party.
  • High levels of bureaucracy stall the process significantly.
  • Over $700 million lost to corruption.
  • Zero confidence in the property market.

Why did the Indian Government use blockchain?

Secure data

Blockchain guarantees that any information can not be arbitrarily amended, altered or or deleted by any single individual.

Transparent

It is easy to track actions made by every system user and define who is specifically responsible for each.

Speed

Operational efficiency is increased hundreds of times.

Key Benefits of the Apla Blockchain

Blockchain 3.0

We use “Smart Law” technology that operates above the level of protocols. Unlike Smart Contracts, Smart Laws can evaluate and rectify errors where there are program contradictions or flaws in the algorithm.

Regulated ecosystem

Rapid development
