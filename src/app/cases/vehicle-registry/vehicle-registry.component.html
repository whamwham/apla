Client

Summary

Apla developed a blockchain app for the Indian government which is able to optimize public sector employee work and increase the speed of registering a vehicle by 20 times. Due to the the masses amount of paperwork, the system took up to 60 days to complete registration. However, our solution allows citizens to pay all of the necessary state charges when purchasing a vehicle in just one click, instantly handing the system the full set of documents required and providing the user with a unique number. As such, the whole registration process can now be reduced to just three days.

The app is integrated with the Amazon Rekognition ID system and this eradicates any identification number conflicts, whilst also allowing the police force to instantly identify drivers using a simple photograph they can take from a smartphone.

Background

Each of India’s vehicle registry departments works solely with paper documents. Registering a vehicle takes a lot of time: up to 2 months. Fraudulent behaviour often takes place, with cases of “migrating” one registration number onto another a common occurrence.

We want to bring transparency and accountability to the system. The transport department is going to be fully computerized. Our main objective is to provide services to people on their doorstep, whilst also discouraging unscrupulous elements.

Sunil Sharma
Minister for Transport

Problems

  • Document verification takes up to two months.
  • Vehicle owners spend additional funds on using intermediary agencies.
  • Cases of fraudulent behaviour on part of the intermediary agencies.
  • Documents can be falsified or lost.
  • Illegal “migration” of registration numbers from one vehicle to another.
  • Not possible for automatic number-plate recognition systems to be used by the police due to the lack of a countrywide standard.

All solved by one solution: Apla

Solution

Seller photographs the buyer and enters the purchase details into a mobile app.

The purchaser’s identity is validated automatically via the Amazon Rekognition facial recognition system.

The information is sent to the transport department.

An appointed official registers the vehicle, issues the license plate and adds the data into the blockchain registry.

Police on the roads can instantly identify the vehicle and its owner by taking a photograph on a smartphone.

Vehicle registration steps

Before: 60 days

Receive a set of over 10 documents from the seller upon purchasing a vehicle.
Show that you are a resident in India.
Receive a confirmation from the police that the vehicle has no criminal history.
Submit a set of documents to the regional transport officer.
Have the vehicle undergo inspection by the relevant authority.
Pay road tax and registration fees.
Receive a unique ID number and registration.

After: 3 days

Paperless process.
Pay for all services in one click.
Automatically submit all the requisite documents in electronic form.
Wait for approval.
Get unique number and registration.

Time reduced up to 95%
